# Brand Name Options

## The Product

A **GPS-powered exploration tool** that wellness, adventure, and luxury properties provide to their guests. Properties create hotspots (points of interest) with rich content, and guests use an interactive map to roam and discover.

**Key features:**
- Embeddable maps for properties
- GPS-based proximity triggers (auto-reveal content within 10m)
- Rich media hotspots (images, audio guides)
- Two modes: tap-to-discover or auto-trigger
- Offline support for remote locations

**The experience:** Guests literally *wander* around the property while discovering content.

---

## Final Three Candidates

### 1. Wandernest (Recommended)

**Etymology:** "Wander" (roam, explore) + "Nest" (home, sanctuary)

**Why it fits this product:**
- Guests *wander* around the *nest* (property)
- The name describes exactly what users do with the map
- Speaks to the guest experience, which is the value prop to properties

**Works across verticals:**
- Safari lodge: "Explore our grounds with Wandernest"
- Wellness retreat: "Wander through our sanctuary"
- Luxury hotel: "Discover our estate at your own pace"
